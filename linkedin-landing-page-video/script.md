# ElevenLabs script

Voice direction:

- British male voice.
- First person, calm and precise.
- Stability: 0.35.
- Clarity and similarity: 0.80.
- Pace: calm, direct, and slightly slower than a typical ad.
- Emotional anchor: quiet leads from unclear pages.

## Phase map

Hook, 0.0s to 2.5s:

One direct recognition hook appears on a clean dark background with no webpage behind it.

Tension, 2.5s to 15.0s:

Show the weak site on the right while a dark diagnosis rail reveals one issue at a time on the left.

Twist, 15.0s to 24.0s:

Reveal the polished landing page mock. Use small callouts and connector lines instead of large overlay captions.

CTA, 24.0s to 30.0s:

Use one clean end card with the $50 down and 48-hour delivery promise, the portfolio URL, and Chuck Baryames underneath.
